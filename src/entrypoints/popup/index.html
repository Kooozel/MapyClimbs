MapyClimbs

No GPX captured yet

Automatically captures GPX exports from Mapy.cz route planner and detects climbs with ProCyclingStats difficulty scoring.

How to use

  1. Open the Route Planner on Mapy.cz
  2. Plan your route
  3. Click MapyClimbs in the route toolbar — the extension fetches & analyzes the GPX silently
  4. Climb cards and map pins appear instantly in the sidebar
